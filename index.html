Personal. Documented. Unaddressed.

As esports training volume increases, the same health indicators that make you better at the game make your body worse.

04 — THE PROPOSAL

Three software extensions.
Same WHOOP. New audience.

No new brand. No new hardware. Three software extensions built on top of WHOOP's existing platform, Whoop Coach AI, and Whoop Unite framework — each tailored to an audience Whoop doesn't currently serve.

01 / GAMER MODE
Personal performance for the competitive player
A new dashboard module within the existing WHOOP app — designed for ranked players who treat their game like a sport.
  • Tilt risk scoring (cortisol-based)
  • Wrist load + RSI prevention
  • Per-game stress profiling
  • Session-aware Whoop Coach
  • Sleep vs. rank correlation
  • Flow window prediction
02 / CREATOR MODE
Streamer health for the always-on creator
A new dashboard module designed for full-time content creators — vocal load, burnout, and live stream biometrics.
  • Vocal health monitoring
  • Burnout calendar tracking
  • Live OBS biometric overlay
  • Viewer retention vs. HR correlation
  • Session intensity heat-mapping
  • Content schedule AI
03 / TEAMS & AGENCY
Whoop Unite extended for esports orgs & talent agencies
The institutional B2B tier — fleet dashboards for esports coaching staffs and creator talent agencies.
  • Esports org coaching dashboards
  • Pre-match readiness alerts
  • Talent agency fleet view
  • Burnout risk monitoring
  • Revenue-at-risk modeling
  • Travel/LAN load planning

07 — THE AUTHOR

Why I built this — and why I'm the right person to walk you through it.

I'm a first-generation Arab American with a biological sciences degree and a background as a competitive Call of Duty player and coach. I've spent the last decade inside the community this pitch is about — as a player, a teammate, a coach watching teenage talent burn out, and a biohacker trying to keep my own performance from collapsing under the volume of competitive play.

As my ranking, placements, and earnings climbed, my physical and mental health declined. More hours in the chair. Less sleep. Higher stress. Better at the game. Worse at everything else. I assumed it was just me — until I started looking at the data.

Every major health database now confirms the same pattern: CDC's NHIS-Teen survey, the NBER mental health analyses, the BMC Sports Science esports cohort studies, the Lancet Commission's 2025 adolescent health report. The crisis is documented, peer-reviewed, and growing. The infrastructure to address it has not been built.

I built this pitch because someone had to build it, and because Will Ahmed's recent tweet about esports adoption confirms that the audience is already coming to WHOOP organically. They just don't have a product designed for who they are. I think WHOOP is the only company positioned to build it — and I think I'm the right person to have helped map it.
